NP Membership Details

Cost: ₦0 / year

  • Free listing in the basic public chamber directory.
  • Access to monthly networking mixers.
  • Standard training resource access.

Bronze Membership Details

Cost: ₦150, 000 / year

  • Standard directory profile with a business logo link.
  • 10% discount on chamber training and seminar entry fees.
  • Access to regional B2B networking hubs.

Silver Membership Details

Cost: ₦300, 000 / year

  • Priority listing tier within the membership directory.
  • 25% discount on event admissions and banner ads.
  • 1 annual spotlight position feature on the main homepage.
  • Invitations to exclusive civic policy roundtables.

Gold Membership Details

Cost: ₦500, 000 / year

  • Top-tier permanent feature positioning on search pages.
  • Free entry to all standard chamber events and training courses.
  • Continuous rolling homepage spotlight features.
  • Private consultations with regional development boards.
