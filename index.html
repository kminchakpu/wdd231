About Me

Hello! I’m Kevin Cross Minchakpu, and I build things with code. Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Software Development at Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho), studying remotely from Abuja, Nigeria. For me, software development isn't just about logic—it's also about creativity. One of my favorite things to do is treat the browser like a gallery, using JavaScript to paint on the HTML5 Canvas. I’m deeply dedicated to mastering new frameworks and languages, always aiming to build software that has a real, positive impact on people's daily lives. The tech industry is evolving fast, and I’m excited to be a part of its future. Let's connect! You can dive into my code and projects by visiting my GitHub Page.
